About Rashi

Hi, I'm Rashi Singh — a certified yoga trainer dedicated to guiding others on a journey of inner peace, strength, and self-discovery. Over the years, I’ve had the blessing of helping 5,000+ individuals reconnect with themselves—physically, mentally, and spiritually. Yoga, for me, is more than a practice—it’s a way of life. Through my carefully designed online sessions, I aim to make this ancient wisdom accessible to all, no matter where you are or what your schedule looks like.

Every class is crafted with intention, blending breath, movement, and mindfulness to create space—not just in the body, but in the soul. Whether you're a beginner or returning to the mat, I invite you to walk this path with me—toward balance, healing, and a deeper sense of self.
